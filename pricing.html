Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Start with a 14-day free trial on any plan. No credit card required. Experience full access to all features during your trial period.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and bank transfers for annual subscriptions.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise plans may include an onboarding fee depending on customization requirements.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly billing. Contact us to switch to annual payments.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You can export all your data at any time. After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days before permanent deletion.

Is training included?

All plans include access to our knowledge base and video tutorials. Enterprise plans include personalized training and onboarding.

Can I get a custom plan?

Yes! If none of our plans fit your needs, contact our sales team to discuss a custom solution tailored to your business.
